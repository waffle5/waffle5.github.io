Intro

Hi, I'm Steven! I'm a passionate, resilient, and tenacious software engineer with a focus on gameplay programming. Ever since creating game development and design diagrams on my notebook at 8 years old, I became captivated by the world of gaming. I began my game development journey while pursuing a Computer Science degree at CSU Long Beach. I have 3+ years of experience in Unity and C#, and 1+ years of experience in Unreal Engine and C++. As a graduate honing my craft with Unreal Engine and C++, I have released and led 2D, 3D, and VR games in Unity/C# under tight deadlines while working with diverse cross-functional teams of various sizes.

Additionally, I've also worked as a full-stack software engineer for Luong Lasting LLC and developed a web platform to optimize their bookkeeping and reduce manual work.
